How does initial problem identification and formulation (vs. the lack of it) affect hack outcomes? Our [working paper](https://papers.ssrn.com/sol3/papers.cfm?abstract_id=3732971) titled "The search for novel product uses: An investigation of IKEA hacks" attempts to theorize and provide evidence in the context of users hacking IKEA products. with Shi-Ying Lim.
