title: Invention Integrality and Team Gender Composition
subtitle: Updated
tags: [technological innovation, collaboration, gender]

We show using both archival data and experiments that teams with at least one female member may create more valuable inventions than all-male teams when the focal invention structure is integral. In collaboration with [Steffen Keck](https://strategy.univie.ac.at/people/steffen-keck/), [Haibo Liu](https://profiles.ucr.edu/app/home/profile/haiboliu), and [Wenjie Tang](https://sites.google.com/view/wenjietang).
