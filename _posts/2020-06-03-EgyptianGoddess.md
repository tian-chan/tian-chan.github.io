Can novel product designs be effectively protected by design rights? Here's a working [piece](https://papers.ssrn.com/sol3/papers.cfm?abstract_id=3574702) with [Paul Hsu](http://qf.site.nthu.edu.tw/p/405-1366-165135,c5738.php) and [Kevin Tseng](https://sites.google.com/site/kevintsengfinance/) showing that the Egyptian Goddess vs. Swisa ruling of 2008, which ruled that designs must "only" be assessed holistically for infringement, helped improve the market value of more complex designs.
