Here's a working [piece](https://papers.ssrn.com/sol3/papers.cfm?abstract_id=3574702) with Paul Hsu and Kevin Tseng showing that the Egyptian Goddess vs. Swisa ruling of 2008, which ruled that designs must "only" be assessed holistically for infringement, helped improve the market value of more complex designs.
